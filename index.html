Overview

Exploring the small winding roads of the beautiful landscape in the vicinity of my hometown is one of my favorite activities in my sparetime, be it with the motorbike, the roadster or a bicycle. I'm especially enthusiastic to classic race-bicycles. This site shows some fine examples of bikes owned by friends and me, in alphabetical order.

  1. Bottecchia
  2. Dancelli
  3. Francesco Moser
  4. KTM




Fine bikes

Bottecchia

Trivia

The company was founded from famous italian racer Ottavio Bottecchia. You will find an interesting article in Wikipedia.

Joe's Bottecchia

Great Bottechia Giro, built probably 1987. A joy to ride, though the shifting with Campagnolo Victory needs to get used to - but that's part of the fun! Well, and you should also get used to the brakes 😉
